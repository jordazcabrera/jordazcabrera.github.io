Mission Learning Elementary School

Mission Learning Elementary School is one of the largest and oldest Elementary Schools in the Bay Area, located in the San Francisco's Mission District. It was founded in 1916 by a group of missionaries committed to academic excellence through a standards-based curriculum. Today, we have a total of 30 classrooms, over 500 students, and a faculty of 50+ professionals and graduates of top universities who are experts in their field of study. We provide free lunches, books, and tuition based on families needs and eligibility.

Throughout the years, we have encouraged our students to achieve academic excellence and to lead a well-balanced life. We teach our students the value of social responsibility and the acceptance of others in this multi-cultural society. We want our students to have a strong foundation in traditional areas of study such as science, math, and language arts and to develop an important set of skills including critical thinking, creativity, communication, problem solving, and collaboration.
